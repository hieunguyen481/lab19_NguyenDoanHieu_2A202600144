# Tech Company Corpus

## doc_001: OpenAI

OpenAI is an artificial intelligence company headquartered in San Francisco. OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, John Schulman, and Wojciech Zaremba. OpenAI develops ChatGPT and GPT models. Microsoft is a major partner and investor in OpenAI through Azure cloud infrastructure.

## doc_002: Microsoft

Microsoft is a technology company headquartered in Redmond. Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft. Microsoft operates in cloud computing, productivity software, gaming, and artificial intelligence. Microsoft develops Azure, Microsoft 365, GitHub Copilot, and Windows. Microsoft partners with OpenAI and uses Azure to host AI services.

## doc_003: Google and Alphabet

Google is a technology company headquartered in Mountain View. Google was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Alphabet is the parent company of Google. Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet and Google. Google develops Gemini, Google Search, Android, and Google Cloud. Google competes with Microsoft and OpenAI in artificial intelligence.

## doc_004: Nvidia

Nvidia is a semiconductor and artificial intelligence computing company headquartered in Santa Clara. Jensen Huang is the CEO and co-founder of Nvidia. Nvidia develops GPUs, CUDA, and AI computing platforms. Nvidia partners with cloud providers such as Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, and Amazon Web Services.

## doc_005: Amazon

Amazon is a technology and e-commerce company headquartered in Seattle. Andy Jassy is the CEO of Amazon. Amazon operates Amazon Web Services, also called AWS, which provides cloud computing infrastructure. AWS competes with Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud. Amazon develops Alexa as an AI assistant.

## doc_006: Meta

Meta is a social technology company headquartered in Menlo Park. Mark Zuckerberg founded Facebook and is the CEO of Meta. Meta develops Llama models, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Quest devices. Meta competes with Google, OpenAI, and Microsoft in artificial intelligence.

## doc_007: Apple

Apple is a consumer technology company headquartered in Cupertino. Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne founded Apple in 1976. Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple. Apple develops iPhone, macOS, iOS, and Apple Intelligence. Apple competes with Google in mobile operating systems through iOS and Android.

## doc_008: Tesla and xAI

Tesla is an electric vehicle and clean energy company headquartered in Austin. Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla. xAI is an artificial intelligence company founded by Elon Musk. xAI develops Grok. xAI competes with OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic in AI assistants.

## doc_009: Anthropic

Anthropic is an artificial intelligence company headquartered in San Francisco. Anthropic was founded by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei. Anthropic develops Claude. Amazon and Google have invested in Anthropic. Anthropic competes with OpenAI and xAI in AI assistants.

## doc_010: IBM

IBM is a technology company headquartered in Armonk. Arvind Krishna is the CEO of IBM. IBM develops Watson and Red Hat OpenShift. IBM operates in enterprise software, hybrid cloud, and artificial intelligence. IBM acquired Red Hat in 2019.
